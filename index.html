Aftercare

  • Day 1–3: Keep the wrap as advised. Wash gently with lukewarm water and fragrance-free soap, pat dry, and apply a thin layer of ointment.
  • Day 4–14: Moisturize 2–3× daily. Avoid soaking, sun, and scratching while it peels.
  • After 2 weeks: Continue moisturizing. Use SPF to protect your tattoo.
