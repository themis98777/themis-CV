Aftercare

  1. Keep the wrap as advised. Wash gently with lukewarm water & fragrance-free soap.
  2. Pat dry; apply a thin layer of recommended ointment.
  3. Days 4–14: moisturize regularly, avoid soaking, scratching, or direct sun.
  4. After healing: keep using SPF to protect the tattoo & preserve contrast.
